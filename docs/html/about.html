About Kasparov

Kasparov room is an initiative focusing on fundamental skills like research and debate among students from Barsha and Garhoud. Students form teams of four representing a nation, defending their allocated region from chaos. With 40 students from Barsha and Garhoud, the initiative includes 10 countries and four conferences throughout the year, allowing students to discuss their nation's status with other members.
